Why a digital ID?

This project combines identity-related records in one platform so authorized users can access the right data quickly.

Government Search

Government users can filter and inspect person records with detailed profile views.

Connected Service Panels

Hospital, marriage/notary, criminal records, passport, and citizen profile pages are all part of the same workflow.

Role-Based Access

Users sign in and are routed to the panel that matches their role and permissions.

How it works

1

Sign in

Use username, email, or phone with your passcode on the login page.

2

Open your assigned panel

After authentication, you are redirected to government, hospital, marriage/notary, police, or citizen panel.

3

Search and manage records

Use the available tools to inspect records and continue official workflows based on your role.

Security built in

Access to every panel is protected by login and role authorization.

Login Required

Users must authenticate before opening protected pages.

Role Permissions

Each account can only use the panels and actions allowed for its role.

Session Safety
