Why a digital ID?

Carry less, prove more. Your digital ID brings the details you need into a clean, scannable format.

Selective Disclosure

Unlike a physical card that shows everything (address, full name, DOB), a Digital ID lets you share only what’s needed.

High-Level Encryption

Physical IDs are easy to fake or steal. Digital IDs use cryptographic signatures and are stored in your phone’s Secure Enclave. Even if someone grabs your phone, they can't access your ID without your FaceID or passcode.

Instant Verification

No more waiting in lines or scanning paper documents. Because it’s linked to government databases in real-time, you can open bank accounts or sign official contracts instantly from your couch.
